Ways You Can Help Animal Care Society

We are always in need of volunteers to help with everything from animal care to paperwork. If you are interested in volunteering at ACS, please stop by during normal business hours.

We are a privately-owned, non-profit animal shelter, meaning we receive no funding from the state. Currently, 70% of our operation costs are supported by donors. Whether you donate $5 or $500, every dollar is greatly appreciated and goes to providing our animals with their daily needs until they find their furever homes.

We are always in need of the following supplies. If you would like to donate any of these, you can drop them off during normal business hours. Please contact us if you have a donation but are unable to come during normal hours.

  • Dog Food (dry kibble or wet canned)
  • Grain-free Dog Food for dogs with allergies
  • Cat Food (dry or canned)
  • Cat litter (non-scoopable)
  • New (or gently-used and washable) pet beds
Re-Tail Thrift Shop is owned and operated by ACS. We sell a wide variety of new and gently-used household items, from furniture to dishes to decor, for great prices! All of the items for sale have been donated by our wonderful supporters. Next time you have a spring cleaning, consider donating your unwanted items to us. All proceeds go directly to ACS.
If you shop at Kroger and have a Plus Card, you can sign up for their Community Rewards program on their website. You can select a local charity of your choice, like Animal Care Society, and Kroger will donate a portion of your total purchases for the year to the specified charity. To donate to ACS, visit Kroger Community Rewards and select Animal Care Society - #10913.
